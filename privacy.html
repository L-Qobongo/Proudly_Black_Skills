Privacy Policy

Welcome to Proudly Black Skills! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, fill out a form, or interact with the platform. This includes your name, email address, and other relevant details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To personalize your experience on the platform.
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and support.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to operate the platform, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system can be completely secure.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our platform. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the platform may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us at privacy@proudlyblackskills.com to make a request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@proudlyblackskills.com.
